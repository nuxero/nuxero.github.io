About Me.

My name is Hector Zelaya. I am from El Salvador, a small (and beautiful) country from Central America. I am a computer software engineer.

Since 2015 I’ve been doing a little bit of everything: working with infrastructure & servers, programming, writing end-to-end tests, blogging and writing “techie” documentation. I love to learn new things every day and of course that I enjoy a good challenge.

Out of the office I like to play the guitar and the piano, I also sing and play video games. I am happily married with the most wonderful woman in the world and I am the father of a smart and beautiful girl.
